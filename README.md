## Game Objectives
Game objectives listed by game and type. This should help you decide whether to include time-consuming or difficult objectives.

### Tiny Bookshop
- Sell (1-25) (random genre) books
- Sell (30-200) books at (random location) | Time for Far Beach
- Purchase (1-16) boxes of books
- Earn (50-1000) coins at (random location) | Time for Far Beach
- Buy (1-12) items at the Flea Market | Time
- Attend the end of season event | Time
- Recommend (5-25) (random genre) books | Time & Difficult
- Sell (30-200) books at (random weekly event) | Time
- Sell (30-100) books in one day | Difficult
- Purchase (1-8) seasonal items | Time & Difficult

### Hades II
- Defeat a (random Boss/Location) with (Random Weapon/Aspect) | Time consuming for full runs; rolled weapons/aspects are chosen by yaml settings
- Defeat a (random Boss/Location) with minimum Fear including at least one rank of (random vow) | Time for full runs; Fear range set by yaml settings
- Complete the (random chaos trial) | Difficult for 4 and 5 ranked trials; Will respect your weapon/aspect choices in yaml for possible trials
- Complete a run of (Chaos Above or Below) | Time; This is also a set option due to the high rng of this mode
- Defeat a Boss with a boon from (Random God) | Time for Hermes; Difficult for Athena; Time & Difficult for Artemis
